# Skill: repair-graph

## Purpose

Repair structural problems in the vault without changing the underlying meaning of source-grounded content.

## Use When

- duplicates are detected
- orphan notes accumulate
- backlink structure is weak
- naming or placement has drifted

## Outputs

- a repair plan
- minimal structural fixes
- unresolved items escalated for evaluator coverage

## Procedure

1. Identify duplicate notes, orphans, broken links, and inconsistent aliases.
2. Propose the smallest safe repair.
3. Preserve provenance, note placement rules, and prior claims.
4. If semantic judgment is needed, prefer leaving the issue unresolved over destructive repair.
5. Feed recurring repair patterns into the evaluator and `pitfalls.md`.

## Guardrails

- structural cleanup must not erase evidence
- repairs must be reversible when possible
- ambiguity is not a license for aggressive consolidation

## Success

The graph becomes easier to navigate without losing trustworthiness.
